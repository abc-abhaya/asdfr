What do we do?

We are a community of Tech enthusiasts who come together to explore the world of Information Technology.
Our activities includes:

Weekly CTF:

Join our weekly Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges to sharpen your cybersecurity skills. Solve puzzles on cryptography, forensics, and web security while learning to defend against cyber threats.

Project development:

Members can work on individual or group projects, ranging from web development and app creation to hardware projects and robotics. We provide guidance, resources, and support to help bring your ideas to life.

Competitions and Challenges:

We participate in local and national IT competitions, giving our members the chance to test their skills against other students and gain recognition for their work.

Fun & Social Events:

We also organize social events like game nights, tech-themed quizzes, and Tech-fest. These activities help build a strong sense of community and make learning fun.
